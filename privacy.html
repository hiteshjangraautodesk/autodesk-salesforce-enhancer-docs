Privacy Policy

How Autodesk Salesforce Enhancer handles information. Last updated: 26 June 2026.

1. Introduction

Autodesk Salesforce Enhancer (“the Extension”) is a Chrome extension for Autodesk employees using Salesforce Lightning, Autodesk Knowledge, and (optionally) ChatGPT for email drafting assistance.

The Extension does not sell user data, show ads, use analytics SDKs, or operate a developer-controlled backend that receives Salesforce case content or email bodies.

2. Who may use the Extension

Use is limited to individuals who complete employee verification with a personal @autodesk.com work email. Shared or generic mailboxes (for example customersupport@autodesk.com) are rejected. Verification data is stored only on your device.

3. Single purpose

The Extension has one purpose: Salesforce and Knowledge productivity for Autodesk support agents — including copy tools, case-tab validation UI, attachment highlighting, and optional user-initiated ChatGPT email assist.

7. Information we do not collect

  • Passwords, MFA codes, or Autodesk session tokens
  • OpenAI / ChatGPT API keys
  • Advertising or analytics identifiers
  • Case numbers, customer PII, or email content on developer servers

8. Sharing and third parties

The Extension developer does not receive your data. When you use Email AI, selected text is copied to your ChatGPT tab. OpenAI’s and Salesforce’s privacy policies apply to those sites. No data is sold to third parties or data brokers.

11. Remote code

No. All Extension code is bundled in the Chrome Web Store package. External websites are not used to download or execute Extension logic.

12. Email AI (optional)

  1. You select text in a Salesforce email composer.
  2. The Extension copies that selection and opens ChatGPT in a popup you control.
  3. You edit content in ChatGPT under OpenAI’s terms.
  4. You click Salesforce in ChatGPT to paste the reply into your email selection.

13. Retention and your choices

  • Sign out — clears stored employee verification
  • Reset in options — restores default toggles
  • Uninstall — removes all local Extension data

14. Children

Not directed at children; intended for Autodesk employees in a work context.
